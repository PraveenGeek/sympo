 About

The success of this brand name OZMENTA has been achieved through the element of consistency. Now entering its ninth consecutive year of glorious success, the events history has accumulated a whooping crowd of an average of over 6000 students from the nook and cranny of the country. We consider the brand 'OZMENTA' as our own every year, as this event is conducted by the students, for the students.This symposium provides a grandeur stage for all the students, of any Engineering discipline, to splurge their ideas and talents in technical and non-technical events.
